Biography of Leo Ogle

Leo E. Ogle was born on September 25, 2004 in Rochester, MN. Since he was a wee lad young he had always enjoyed serving, and had aspired to do good for all. He has lived in five different houses over 13 years, so you could say he has a pretty diverse background. Since Leo has been going to school at Our Lady of Grace, he has always seen something lacking in our student council. That is respect for the student body. Our student council has consistently bent the knee to the administration, but that will no longer go on, if you elect Leo Ogle as your Student Council President, vote Ogle for office.
