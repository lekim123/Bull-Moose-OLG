Biography of Leo Ogle

Leo is running for the position of Male Student Council President.

Leo E. Ogle was born on September 25, 2004 in Rochester, MN. Since he was a wee lad he had always enjoyed serving, and had aspired to do good for all. He has lived in five different houses over 13 years, so you could say he has a pretty diverse background. Our student council has consistently bent the knee to the administration, but that will no longer go on, if you elect Leo Ogle as your Student Council President, vote Ogle for office.
